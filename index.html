B.Tech in Computer Science

B.Tech in Computer Science is a 4-year undergraduate engineering course on practical and theoretical knowledge of computer hardware and software. The minimum eligibilty for this course requires passing class 12th in Physics,Mathematics and Computer science with minimum 50% marks.

B.Tech in Electrical

It is a undergraduate Electrical Engineering course. Electriacl Engineering is a field of engineering that generally deals with the study and application of electricity,electronics and electromagnetism. Electrical engineering is considered to deal with the problems associated withlarge-scale electrical systems such as power transmission and motor control, wheereas electrical enggineering deals with the study of small scale electronic system.
