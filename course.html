B.Tech in Computer Science

B.Tech in Computer Science is a 4-year undergraduate engineering course on practical and theoretical knowledge of computer hardware and software. The minimum eligibilty for this course requires passing class 12th in Physics,Mathematics and Computer science with minimum 50% marks.VTU's B.Tech in Computer Science is three years Full-Time degree programme which is designed to develop a student's knowledge of computer science from the basics of high level language programming. We provide well structured labs and equipments for the students. We have lectures who have completed Ph.D in respective subjects. Monthly internal assessment are one to know the level of understanding of course.Department organises regular visits to reputed industries and power stations. The students are encouraged to publish project work ion reputed national/international conferences. Our placed students work in several comapanie like wipro, infosys, amazon, accenture. we provide training for placement from second semester itself and we have placement cell which helps students in improving their skill and vocabulary.

B.Tech in Electrical

It is a undergraduate Electrical Engineering course. Electriacl Engineering is a field of engineering that generally deals with the study and application of electricity,electronics and electromagnetism. Electrical engineering is considered to deal with the problems associated withlarge-scale electrical systems such as power transmission and motor control, wheereas electrical enggineering deals with the study of small scale electronic system.B.Tech in Electrical Engineering at VTU offers a unique mix of electrical ,electronics and computer related courses enabling the students to take up professional career/higher studies in any of these areas. The curriculum based on credit scheme is updated periodically to reflect the changes in the energy managemnent. Department has excellent link with leading industries and has a well connected alumini network. Department organises regular visits to reputed industries and power stations. The students are encouraged to publish project work ion reputed national/international conferences. To join this course you have to score minimum of 60% in 12th exam and should score 20,000 below rank.
